Frequently Asked Questions


1. How many team members can we have in our team?
You are allowed to have a maximum of 4 in your team.

Yes . However , teams with multiple co-founders are usually able to accelerate their progress in a much better way.

3. Do you fund travel and accommodation costs for the final round? Is accommodation provided?
Travel and accommodation costs need to be borne by the participants. Accommodation will be provided in our campus itself , and we will ensure to do it at a minimal cost.

4. What can I expect from the mentoring sessions?
In the mentoring sessions, the ideas will be able to get feedback from high profile mentors who with their experience and knowledge will suggest ways to improve their prototype , pitching skills, etc.
